About Me

I am a French gameplay programmer student and young father always eager to learn more!

Precociously oriented towards mechanics, bodywork and car painting, I spent 7 years in a semi-professional environment that didn't satisfy my ambition and my taste for reflection. As a result, I reoriented myself in an environment that fulfilled me. As a fan of online pc games, the reflexion and logic that I found in these games fitted in perfectly with what a programmer's job requires, and I immediately liked it as i started my studies.
Since then, I've done a 3-year degree at a video game and game design school where I learned how to use various software and programming languages as well as many other game-related skills. I then passed the entrance exam to the JMIN Master at the French prestigious school Cnam-Enjmin, where I'm now in my second year of a master's degree to finish my education.
